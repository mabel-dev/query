Query Editor

Only a subset of the DQL (Data Query Language) is supported.

  • SELECT
  • FROM
  • WHERE
  • GROUP BY
  • ORDER BY
  • LIMIT

NOTE: as at time of writing help, JOINs and HAVING were not implemented

Filter Operations:

  • equals (=), also as '=='
  • not equals (<>), also as '!='
  • greater than (>)
  • greater than or equal (>=)
  • less than (<)
  • less than or equal (<=)
  • is (IS)
  • isn't (IS NOT)
  • SQL like (LIKE)
  • not SQL like (NOT LIKE)

Aggregation Functions:

  • COUNT
  • SUM
  • MAX
  • MIN

Scalar Functions:

  • DAY
  • MONTH
  • YEAR
  • DATE
  • UCASE
  • LCASE
  • LEN
  • ROUND
